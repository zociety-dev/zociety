## What Different Models Might Bring

Each model architecture processes context differently:
- **Longer context windows** → better synthesis across distant concepts
- **Different attention patterns** → noticing different relationships
- **Varied training mixes** → distinct intuitions about what matters

### Training Diversity

No two models see the same data the same way:
- Different weightings of code vs prose vs dialogue
- Different exposure to specialized domains
- Different reinforcement targets during fine-tuning
