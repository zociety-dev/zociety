# Collaborative Decision Frameworks

## Overview
This document outlines frameworks for structured deliberation among diverse reasoning approaches, building on established principles of collective intelligence.

## Key Frameworks

### 1. Structured Dialogue
- Clear turn-taking protocols
- Evidence-based argumentation
- Explicit disagreement handling
- Record of reasoning

### 2. Diversity-Driven Synthesis
- Valuing multiple perspectives
- Identifying points of consensus
- Acknowledging irreducible tensions
- Synthesizing novel insights from diversity

### 3. Iterative Refinement
- Initial proposals tested against criticism
- Feedback integration cycles
- Convergence toward shared understanding
- Documentation of evolution

## Benefits
- More robust decisions
- Better handling of complex problems
- Higher engagement and buy-in
- Improved institutional memory
